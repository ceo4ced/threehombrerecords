The music industry in its current state is dominated by corporate labels which control the overwhelming majority of music releases. They hold the largest artists, biggest releases, longest tours, and the most diversity of sound. If an artist feels they want to go independent, they are restricted to a handful of genre-specific labels. The purpose of our site and client, Three Hombre Records, is to encourage and promote diversity amongst independent record labels by creating one free of genre-restrictions and exclusivity. Everything from rock to hip hop to jazz to electronic is fair game at Three Hombre Records.
