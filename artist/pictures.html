About Pictures

Formed in 2011, Pictures met through their high school band class in suburban Durham, NC. Following a few years of on-and-off playing as they finished their studies, they took the band full time in 2013. It was not until 2014 with the release of their single Course Bottom, however, that they began to settle on upbeat progressive metal as their genre of choice. Taking influence from artists like The Helix Nebula and Sithu Aye, Pictures combines djenty-progressive metal alongside heavy use of programmed virtual instruments to create a pseudo-soundtrack feel. Following the release of their 2016 single Malaise with solo artist Plini (ex-Halcyon), Pictures made the transition from instrumental to vocally-fronted. Their debut full-length is set to release in early 2019, marking their first Three Hombre Records release.
