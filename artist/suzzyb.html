About SuzzyB and her band...

Growing up in the South, SuzzyB started playing piano at age 6. Her mother, a band teacher emphasized the love of music which lead to Suzzy learning how to play ten instruments. From her Hip Hop tranisient ambient beats to her jazzy riffs, SuzzyB has made a name for herself in the electronic ambient world. At age 21, she still has rooms to grow.
