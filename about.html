3 Hombre Records

1234 Franklin Street, Chapel Hill, NC 27514

919-555-1212 For more information, rates, and booking details: info@3hombre.com



3 Hombre Records is a group of musically-oriented entrepreneurs based out of Chapel Hill, North Carolina. We met during our time at UNC Chapel Hill and formed a bond over our love for music. Though our tastes may be different, the universal language drew us in together to put our combined love of the art to use. Since forming, 3 Hombre Records has became the premiere independent label in the Triangle region, doing everything from hosting local talent demonstrations to signing the best artists coming from the area. We pride ourselves in being genre-inclusive, defying the norm of stylistically-similar independent labels.
